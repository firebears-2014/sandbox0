# Apple Sauce

* 5 apples
* 14 gallons water
* 1 knife
* 29 tbsp sugar
* 3 tsp cinnamon

1. Train apples to sit, fetch, and jump in bowl
2. Make apples jump in bowl where you stab them with the knife
3. Calibrate Oven to 500 degrees C
4. Add all ingredients to bowl
5. Cook until solid
6. Leave standing for 2-3 days
